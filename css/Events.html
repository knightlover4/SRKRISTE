EVENTS

WELCOME TO OUR EVENTS PAGE!

Embark on a transformative journey with ISTE, seamlessly merging self-discovery and skill enrichment. Our commitment revolves around nurturing both creative and technical talents. Imagine our workshops, seminars, and webinars as a vibrant palette, offering a myriad of colors to paint your educational experience. Each event contributes a unique hue to the masterpiece of your knowledge and skill acquisition. NIPUNA, a standout within ISTE, serves as a transformative beacon, offering students a plethora of events that extend beyond being mere skills acquisition. It leaves an indelible mark on participants, providing exciting opportunities to engage with the forefront of technology, innovation, and creation. Through the SAHARA social welfare program, we actively contribute to fostering positive societal transformation and community development, striving towards the overarching goal of cultivating a prosperous and harmonious society. A warm welcome awaits those seeking self-exploration, with events contributing to both career development and personal growth. ISTE is more than an organization; it's a community where connections flourish, and meaningful experiences unfold. Join us, connect with like-minded individuals, and confidently stride towards career and personal development. The doors of ISTE are wide open for those ready to embrace the transformative power of exploration and learning.
