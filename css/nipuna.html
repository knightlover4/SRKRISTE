NIPUNA

WELCOME TO NIPUNA PAGE!

NIPUNA, the National Level Techno- Management Symposium, organized by the ISTE student chapter at SRKREC for almost three decades, stands as a symbol of excellence and innovation. Students eagerly participate in this annual event, utilizing the dynamic platform to showcase their unique talents and capabilities. NIPUNA stands out from the traditional classroom setting. By integrating workshops, seminars, technical and non-technical competitions, it offers an immersive learning experience. In the midst of this enriching environment, participants cultivate critical thinking, refine problem-solving skills, and gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology. Nipuna's events offer a distinctive and extraordinary experience, elevating them above the ordinary. Participants not only learn and collaborate but also engage in friendly competition across diverse events. Nipuna, as a visionary knowledge nexus, meticulously cultivates a culture of boundless creativity, ensuring the fluid dissemination of knowledge. This commitment contributes to the forging of academic pillars that sculpt revolutionary leaders and innovators set to leave an indelible mark on the future.

NIPUNA EVENTS

Presentational Events

Step into Nipuna's refined presentation realm, your stage to exhibit communication mastery! Highlight your eloquence, influential approaches and seamless delivery on this stage. Participate in tailored challenges to convey ideas across diverse platforms with precision. Hone your skills in public speaking, persuasion, and impactful presentations with guidance from experienced speakers.

Artistic Events

Discover the arena of innovation in the Artistic Skills section at Nipuna, where imagination meets allure! Unleash your creativity through exceptional styles, from painting and photography to sculpting. Showcase your talents and explore different forms of capturing insights. Portray yourself, delve into aesthetics, and develop your expressive touch. Unveil new captivating prospects by building relationships with other artists and mentors in the art industry.

Managerial Events

Step into Nipuna's Gateway to Managerial Excellence! Engage in our exclusive events tailored to fortify decision-making, amplify strategic thinking and refine leadership finesse. Explore specialized financial insights to broaden your expertise. Join us in the expedition through the complexities of leadership in today's fast-paced society.

Literature Events

Welcome to Nipuna's Literary Arena, dedicated to conquering the craft of storytelling and refining linguistic elegance. Discover handcrafted exercises fostering narrative expertise, language detail, and persuasive skills. Immerse yourself in our thriving network, navigating through the diverse aspects of wordcraft and the complexity of tales, nurturing collaborative learning and imaginative growth.

Technical Events

Venture into the milieu of advancement and technology within the Technical realm at Nipuna, where the future is engineered! Test your technical acumen, problem-solving abilities, and innovation mindset through hands-on challenges and competitions. Explore various technological domains, collaborate with peers, and innovate solutions to real-world problems. Engage with experts and mentors to sharpen your skills, expand your knowledge, and push the boundaries of innovation.
