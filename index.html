About ISTE


Indian society for technical education (ISTE) is a national level nonprofitable society which was started in the year 1941 as Association of Principals of Technical Instistutions (APTI).
In 1968, it was converted to ISTE.
The Primary goal of the ISTE is to offer high-quality training programs for teachers in technical institutions enabling them to enhance thier knowledge and skills within thier respective areas.
This, inturn helps them play a crucial role in nurturing and contributing to the growth of skilled professional engineers that are in demand by various industries.


Departments in ISTE

ISTE has 5 departments

Photoshop Department

The Photoshop department functions as a digital forge, molding raw images into refined compositions. It is a language spoken by those who seek to translate creativity through exploration with various techniques captivating into visual wonders.

Web Department

The Web Department isn't just a team of coders, it's a collective force shaping digital experiences, where ideas get transformed into interactive and engaging websites. Their knowledge across various programming languages enables the implementation of innovative features that elevate the overall digital journey.

VFX Department

The VFX department emerges as a collaborative epicenter for creative minds, collectively entrusted with the mission to elevate visual elements. It entails the skillful fusion of technical expertise and creative imagination for the VFX department to bring fantastical worlds, creatures, and events to life on screen.

Content Department

The content department functions as an imaginative hub, seamlessly transforming ideas into captivating narratives. This department not only focuses on content creation, it also extends to engaging, innovative and showcasing disciplined mastery of language that forges profound connections and leaves a lasting impact on the audience.

Art Department

The art department is a journey into the realm where design melds with feelings. This department often includes painting, handicrafts and creative art models. Here, the focus lies on nurturing the mind to think, the heart to express, and the hand to craft unique works of art, inviting individuals to prioritize innovation and creativity within themselves.

Membership Benefits


ISTE New Delhi will recognize you as a member of a national professional society and will issue membership cards accordingly.
You'll be part of a large academic community with over 1,24,000 professionals, receiving priority access to short-term training for academic improvement and career advancement opportunities.
You can engage in academic events at the chapter, section, and national levels, as well as join activities hosted by prestigious institutions like IITs and NITs.
You can make use of exclusive discounts on the participation fee during the annual fest "NIPUNA."
